V2M — Turn Motion Into Music

A personal project that converts video and live streams into MIDI using computer vision.

(No downloads yet — just sharing experiments.)

How it works

Load a video or camera → YOLO detects objects → DeepSORT tracks them → Axis crossings become MIDI notes.
